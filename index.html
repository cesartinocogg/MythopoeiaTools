The Mythopoet

What is a Mythopoet?

A mythopoet is a creator of mythic worlds — a weaver of legends, geographies, and cosmologies. Unlike a traditional writer, the mythopoet builds the entire stage: the cultures, the magic, the timelines, and the unspoken rules that make a world feel alive.

They are part historian, part geographer, part theologian, and part storyteller. Their craft is not just about plotting a narrative, but about constructing a living, breathing universe that can host infinite stories.
